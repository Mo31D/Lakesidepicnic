Luxury Lakes Ice Cream

Based in the heart of the beautiful Lake District, English Lakes Ice Cream is a family business that has been trading for over two decades. Throughout this time, we have been producing luxury, traditional ice cream using the best possible ingredients. We firmly believe that our Double Jersey Ice Creams, Tangy Real Fruit Sorbets and our Vegan Ice Creams are the very best you can possibly eat. The intense flavours combined with a sublime texture ensures each mouthful is a highly rewarding experience.

Romney's Kendal Mint Cake

Crafted on the edge of the Lake District for over a century, George Romney Ltd is a fourth-generation family business with a proud tradition of sweet-making. First created in 1918 by Sam T. Clarke, our famous Kendal Mint Cake is still made using time-honoured methods and original recipes. Famously carried on the first successful ascent of Everest, it’s a true taste of history. Today, we continue to produce our signature mint cake, rich fudges, and treacle toffee — all lovingly made in Kendal.
