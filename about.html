About Lakeside Picnic

Nestled in the heart of the Lake District, Lakeside Picnic is more than just a café—it’s a little haven for locals and visitors alike. Since opening our doors in March 2015, we’ve been proud to offer a cozy spot where people can relax, enjoy stunning lakeside views, and treat themselves to the best of Cumbria’s local flavors. At our core, we’re all about quality and connection. Whether you're here for a scoop (or two) of Luxury Lakes Ice Cream, a freshly brewed cup of coffee, or a bite of something homemade and delicious, we want every visit to feel like a breath of fresh air. Our ice cream—sourced from the award-winning Luxury Lakes Ice Cream in Kendal—is a real highlight. Made with organic local milk and cream, it's available in a variety of mouthwatering flavors (many gluten-free and all free from artificial additives). Favorites include Thunder & Lightning, Kendal Mint Cake, Raspberry Pavlova, and Cartmel Sticky Toffee Pudding. We also showcase a selection of locally made snacks, drinks, and unique gifts that celebrate the spirit of the Lakes. Whether you're popping in for a quick treat or sitting outside to soak up the view, we’re here to make it special. Thanks for visiting—we can’t wait to welcome you in person!
