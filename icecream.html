Luxury Lakes Ice Cream

Pure Indulgence! The ultimate in luxury ice cream, silky and smooth and packed full of flavour! Our luxury ice cream range is of the highest quality which includes our Great Taste Award winning ice creams, Double Jersey, Thunder & Lightning and Raspberry Pavlova, Cartmel Sticky Toffee Pudding, Belgian Biscuit, Blackcurrant and Cream, Crushed Strawberry and Rum and Raisin. This decadent range is based on a perfect balance of local whole milk, real Jersey or local double cream, rich butter and the finest quality fruits and inclusions.

Double Jersey

Simply made with fresh whole milk, Jersey Double cream and butter. No flavourings, colourings, just a deeply creamy ice cream.
