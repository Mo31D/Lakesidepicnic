Romney's Kendal Mint Cake

For over a century, Romney's has been crafting Kendal Mint Cake—a confection that has become synonymous with adventure and endurance. Originating in 1918 when Sam T. Clarke began production in Kendal, this mint-infused energy bar gained legendary status after accompanying Sir Edmund Hillary and Tenzing Norgay to the summit of Mount Everest in 1953. Today, Romney's continues to produce this iconic treat using traditional methods, offering a taste of history in every bite. At Lakeside Picnic in Ambleside, we proudly stock a variety of Romney's Mint Cake products, inviting you to experience a piece of Lakeland heritage.
